Games of Life

Generations: 1

Explore variations of John Conway's famous Game of Life and see how the grid evolves over time.

You can also make your own rule: Birth (B)) is the number of live neighbors required for a cell to be birthed.

Survival (S) is the number of live neighbors required for a live cell to survive.

Using this notation, Conway's original rule is: B3S23

Draw cells on the grid, or play with the density to randomize the cells.
